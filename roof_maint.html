Residential Roof Maintenance Program

Your home is one of the largest investments you typically have. Your roof not only protects that investment but also plays a part in your homes beauty (inside and outside). Are you ensuring that your investment is protected and maintained to avoid unexpected, costly problems?

Your roof may not be that old or it may be nearing its life expectancy but like all things your roof will require maintenance. Damage to your roof could occur in a number of ways that are never seen until it is too late. Buckling shingles, drought, cracked pipe flashings, ice dams, gutters, animal and pest damage to name a few. Undetected damage and leaks could cause significant damage to insulation, walls, wood rot etc.

The National Roofing Contractors Association suggests that a roof should be inspected at least twice annually. Also, some membrane warranties require regular maintenance for the warranty to stay effective. Warranties cover material performance and workmanship during installation. Maintenance is the process of preserving or keeping something in good condition. To ensure your investment is protected and maintained, Gladiator Roofing and Construction is offering a Residential Roof Maintenance Program at an affordable monthly or annual cost.

The Residential Roof Maintenance Program includes:

  • Two inspections annually
  • Clean debris from roof pans/gutters/drains
  • Inspect and caulk all flashings as needed
  • Seal all roof penetrations as needed
  • Repair nail pops and secure loose shingles as needed
  • Priority scheduling in the event of a leak or storm emergency

Our program is for residential composition and wood roofs only and roof pitches up to 8/12 pitch (pricing adjustments for roofs with steeper pitches). Scheduling is weather permitting and may be subject to change. A written estimate will be provided for any additional or major repairs necessary.

With proper roof maintenance you can relax and have peace of mind. You will have documentation on the performance of the roof, a feature to potential buyers when selling. Protection from fraudulent, fly by night companies during storms by having an established relationship with a roofer you can trust. We will place a sign in the yard helping you to avoid bothersome salesmen and harassment. These are just a few of the benefits to the program.
